History

TechCon was founded in 2010 as a small gathering of technology enthusiasts. Over the years, it has grown into one of the most anticipated global conferences for innovators, startups, and industry leaders. Each year, thousands of participants join to learn, network, and shape the future of technology.

Mission

Our mission is to inspire and empower the tech community by showcasing groundbreaking innovations, fostering collaboration, and encouraging diversity and inclusion in technology. TechCon 2024 continues this mission by offering sessions, workshops, and opportunities for growth and connection.

Past Speakers

Over the years, TechCon has hosted many visionary leaders and innovators, including:

Dr. Ada Lovelace – Pioneering computer scientist, known for her work on early algorithms and inspiring women in tech worldwide.

Elon Musk – Entrepreneur and innovator, whose vision for space exploration and sustainable energy continues to inspire millions.

Grace Hopper – Legendary programmer and U.S. Navy rear admiral, recognized for her contributions to early computer programming languages.
